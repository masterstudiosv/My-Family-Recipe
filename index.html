My Family Lasagna

Traditional Recipe with Love

By: Marvin Josue Borja Hurtado | B2
⏱️
Total Time
45-60 min
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
Servings
6-8 people
⭐
Difficulty
Intermediate

🛒 Ingredients

Pasta & Base

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Olive oil
  • Butter

Meat Sauce

  • Ground beef
  • Garlic cloves
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes/sauce
  • Fresh basil
  • Sugar

Cheese & Sauce

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper

👨‍🍳 Steps

Prep the vegetables

First, peel and chop onions and garlic. Cut tomatoes into small pieces.

Brown the meat

Next, heat olive oil, add onions and garlic, then brown the ground beef.

Make tomato sauce

Then, add tomatoes, sugar, salt, pepper, and basil. Cook 10-15 minutes.

Prepare white sauce

Next, melt butter, add flour, slowly add milk while stirring until thick.

Layer assembly

Then, layer pasta, meat sauce, white sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat.

Bake to perfection

Finally, top with parmesan and bake 25-30 minutes until golden.

💡 Grandma's Tips

  • Let lasagna rest 10 minutes before cutting
  • Prepare sauce day before for better flavor
  • Add red wine to meat sauce for richness
  • Extra flour thickens watery sauce

🍽️ The Final Result

Golden, bubbly, and perfect - ready to share with family!
