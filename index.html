Homemade Lasagna - Recipe Summary

👨‍🍳 Author

Marvin Josue Borja Hurtado | B2

📊 Recipe Information

  • Total Time: 45–60 minutes
  • Servings: 6–8 people
  • Difficulty: Intermediate

🛒 Ingredients (by category)

  • Pasta & Base: Lasagna sheets, olive oil, butter
  • Meat Sauce: Ground beef, garlic, onions, tomatoes, basil, sugar
  • White Sauce & Cheese: Flour, milk, mozzarella, parmesan
  • Seasonings: Salt and black pepper

👣 Steps

  1. 🔪 Chop garlic, onions, and tomatoes
  2. 🔥 Brown ground beef with garlic and onions
  3. 🍅 Simmer tomato sauce with basil and seasoning
  4. 🥄 Make the white sauce with flour, butter, and milk
  5. 📏 Assemble the layers: pasta, meat, white sauce, cheese
  6. ♨️ Bake until golden and bubbly

💡 Grandma’s Tips

  • Let the lasagna rest 10 minutes before cutting
  • Make the sauce the day before for better flavor
  • If it's too watery, add a bit more flour
  • Add a little red wine to the meat sauce for richer taste
