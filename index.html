üõí Ingredients

For the pasta and base

  • A pack of lasagna sheets
  • A little olive oil
  • Some butter

For the meat sauce

  • Some ground beef
  • A few garlic cloves
  • Some onions
  • Many tomatoes (or tomato sauce)
  • A few basil leaves
  • A little sugar

For the white sauce and cheese

  • Some flour
  • A little milk
  • Some mozzarella cheese
  • Some parmesan cheese

Seasonings

  • Some salt
  • Some black pepper

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Steps

1

Initial preparation

First, peel and chop the onions and garlic. Cut the tomatoes into small pieces.

2

Prepare the base

Next, heat a little olive oil in a pan and add the chopped onions and garlic. Cook for a few minutes, then add the ground beef. Mix well until the meat is browned.

3

Create the tomato sauce

Then, add the chopped tomatoes (or tomato sauce), a little sugar, some salt, some black pepper, and a few basil leaves. Let it cook for 10‚Äì15 minutes to make a delicious sauce.

4

Prepare white sauce

Next, prepare the white sauce: melt some butter in a pan, add some flour, mix well, and slowly pour a little milk while stirring until the sauce becomes thick.

5

Layer assembly

Then, in a baking dish, place a layer of lasagna sheets, then add a layer of meat sauce, a layer of white sauce, and some mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until the dish is full.

6

Baking

Finally, sprinkle some parmesan cheese on top and bake for 25‚Äì30 minutes until golden and bubbly. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

üí° Grandma's Tips

  • Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before cutting to maintain its shape
  • You can prepare the sauce the day before for better flavor
  • If it's too watery, add a little more flour to the white sauce
  • For extra flavor, add a little red wine to the meat sauce
